Kobe Bryant

Legend of the Mamba

Kobe Bean Bryant (born August 23, 1978) is an American professional basketball player for the Los Angeles Lakers of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He entered the NBA directly from high school, and he has played for the Lakers his entire career, winning five NBA championships.

"The most important thing is to try and inspire people so that they can be great in whatever they want to do."

- Kobe Bryant
